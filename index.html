Prospect Park Boathouse

Getting to the Boathouse

Enter at Willink Drive and Ocean Avenue. The boathouse is a 5 minute walk from the entrace. Uniformed park staff will direct you.

Taxi: Heading south on Flatbush Ave, turn right into park entrance at Willink Drive before light at Ocean Ave/Empire Blvd intersection.

Subway: Q train to the Prospect Park station. Exit at Flatbush Ave/Ocean Ave at southwest corner exit. Turn left, walk towards the traffic light at Ocean Ave. Cross to park entrance.
